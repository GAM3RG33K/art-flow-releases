Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025
Our Commitment: Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Overview

Art Flow ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile application that allows users to browse and use AI-generated images from Civitai as wallpapers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Currently Collect

We currently do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information outside of your device.

The app may store the following information locally on your device only:

  • App preferences and filter settings
  • Cached images for performance improvement
  • App usage data for functionality purposes

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Contact lists or other personal data

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses images from Civitai.com through their public API. When you use our app:

  • Image requests are made directly to Civitai's servers
  • We do not track or store which images you view
  • Civitai may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices
  • We recommend reviewing Civitai's Privacy Policy

5. Future Changes We May Implement

We may add the following features in future versions:

  • Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience (e.g., which features are most popular, crash reports)
  • Advertisements: We may display advertisements to support app development

Important: If we implement any of these changes, we will update this privacy policy and notify users before collecting any new data.

6. Data Security

Since we don't currently collect personal data, there's minimal security risk. However, we implement the following security measures:

  • All data stays on your device
  • Secure connections (HTTPS) for all external API calls
  • No user accounts or login systems
  • Regular security updates

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app includes content filtering options to help ensure age-appropriate content.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's currently no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear the app's cache and data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for major changes
  • Posting updates on our website

10. International Users

Our app is available worldwide. Since we don't currently collect personal data, international data transfer regulations don't apply. However, please note that Civitai (our image source) may have different data practices for international users.
